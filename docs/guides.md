# Guides - OpenClaw Launch

OpenClaw Launch helps teams choose a model, bind a channel, pay for a plan, track provisioning, and operate OpenClaw instances from a hosted console.

## Before launch review steps

Decide the channel, owner, model route, support mailbox, fallback plan, and token-handling policy before payment.

## Channel token safety

Submit only tokens you are authorized to use and rotate credentials if a channel binding changes hands.

## When to self-host

Stay self-hosted when custom networking, dependency control, or internal platform policy matters more than speed to first launch.

## Pricing and Support

Plans cover deployment capacity, console access, channel setup, runtime tracking, upgrade help, and support through the public pricing flow.
